Frequently asked questions about Los Angeles, California appraisers

How do I choose an art appraiser in Los Angeles, California?

Look for professionals with ISA, ASA, or AAA credentials and USPAP-compliant reports. Start with our vetted directory, schedule a consultation, and ask about recent work that mirrors your collection.

What services do appraisers in Los Angeles, California provide?

Specialists in Los Angeles, California routinely assist with Art appraisal services, Insurance appraisals, Estate and donation appraisals, Insurance Valuation, Estate Tax Appraisals, and Donation Appraisals. Many also support authenticity research, market analysis, and sale preparation for private collectors and institutions.

How much does an art appraisal cost in Los Angeles, California?

Fees vary by scope. Local providers reference Contact for pricing information, Project-based, and $350/hour or $2,500/day plus travel. Expect a written report that complies with USPAP standards and documents methodology, comparables, and appraiser credentials.
