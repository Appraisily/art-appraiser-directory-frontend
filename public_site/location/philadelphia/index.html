Frequently asked questions about Philadelphia, PA appraisers

How do I choose an art appraiser in Philadelphia, PA?

Look for professionals with ISA, ASA, or AAA credentials and USPAP-compliant reports. Start with our vetted directory, schedule a consultation, and ask about recent work that mirrors your collection.

What services do appraisers in Philadelphia, PA provide?

Specialists in Philadelphia, PA routinely assist with USPAP-compliant valuations, Remote evaluations via photo/video submissions, Auction-based appraisals, Estate and collection valuations, Jewelry certification, and Appraisals. Many also support authenticity research, market analysis, and sale preparation for private collectors and institutions.

How much does an art appraisal cost in Philadelphia, PA?

Fees vary by scope. Local providers reference Clear upfront fees; exact rates not listed, Commission-based consignment fees; auction commissions apply, and Approximately $175+ per jewelry item and $100+ for watches; discounts available for multiple items. Expect a written report that complies with USPAP standards and documents methodology, comparables, and appraiser credentials.
