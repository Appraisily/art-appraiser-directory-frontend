Frequently asked questions about Richmond, VA appraisers

How do I choose an art appraiser in Richmond, VA?

Look for professionals with ISA, ASA, or AAA credentials and USPAP-compliant reports. Start with our vetted directory, schedule a consultation, and ask about recent work that mirrors your collection.

What services do appraisers in Richmond, VA provide?

Specialists in Richmond, VA routinely assist with Insurance appraisals, Estate valuations, Auction estimates, Estate evaluations, Damage claims, and Expert witness. Many also support authenticity research, market analysis, and sale preparation for private collectors and institutions.

How much does an art appraisal cost in Richmond, VA?

Fees vary by scope. Local providers reference Insurance appraisals ($200-$500/item), Contact for pricing information, and Tax-deductible gift valuations ($150-$300/item). Expect a written report that complies with USPAP standards and documents methodology, comparables, and appraiser credentials.
