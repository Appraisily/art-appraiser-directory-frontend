Frequently asked questions about Seattle, Washington appraisers

How do I choose an art appraiser in Seattle, Washington?

Look for professionals with ISA, ASA, or AAA credentials and USPAP-compliant reports. Start with our vetted directory, schedule a consultation, and ask about recent work that mirrors your collection.

What services do appraisers in Seattle, Washington provide?

Specialists in Seattle, Washington routinely assist with Art Appraisals, Art Sales, Corporate Art Consulting, Art appraisal services, Insurance appraisals, and Estate and donation appraisals. Many also support authenticity research, market analysis, and sale preparation for private collectors and institutions.

How much does an art appraisal cost in Seattle, Washington?

Fees vary by scope. Local providers reference Contact for pricing information, $5 per item during shows, and Varies (online appraisals generally range from $75-$300+). Expect a written report that complies with USPAP standards and documents methodology, comparables, and appraiser credentials.
