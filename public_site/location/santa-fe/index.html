How do I choose an art appraiser in Santa Fe, NM?

Look for professionals with ISA, ASA, or AAA credentials and USPAP-compliant reports. Start with our vetted directory, schedule a consultation, and ask about recent work that mirrors your collection.

What services do appraisers in Santa Fe, NM provide?

Specialists in Santa Fe, NM routinely assist with Art appraisal, Expert consultation, Educational services, Valuation for estate and insurance purposes, Online art appraisal, and Certification. Many also support authenticity research, market analysis, and sale preparation for private collectors and institutions.

How much does an art appraisal cost in Santa Fe, NM?

Fees vary by scope. Local providers reference Contact for pricing information, Starts at $150; expedited services available with additional fees, and $350/hour or $2,500/day plus expenses.
