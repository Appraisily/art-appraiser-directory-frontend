Frequently asked questions about Columbus, OH appraisers

How do I choose an art appraiser in Columbus, OH?

Look for professionals with ISA, ASA, or AAA credentials and USPAP-compliant reports. Start with our vetted directory, schedule a consultation, and ask about recent work that mirrors your collection.

What services do appraisers in Columbus, OH provide?

Specialists in Columbus, OH routinely assist with Art appraisal, Estate appraisal, Auction services, Expert witness services, Insurance appraisals, and Estate appraisals. Many also support authenticity research, market analysis, and sale preparation for private collectors and institutions.

How much does an art appraisal cost in Columbus, OH?

Fees vary by scope. Local providers reference Custom quotes, $175–$225/hour (non-regulated vs IRS-regulated), and Varies by item complexity. Expect a written report that complies with USPAP standards and documents methodology, comparables, and appraiser credentials.
