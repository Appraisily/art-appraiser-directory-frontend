Frequently asked questions about Minneapolis, MN appraisers

How do I choose an art appraiser in Minneapolis, MN?

Look for professionals with ISA, ASA, or AAA credentials and USPAP-compliant reports. Start with our vetted directory, schedule a consultation, and ask about recent work that mirrors your collection.

What services do appraisers in Minneapolis, MN provide?

Specialists in Minneapolis, MN routinely assist with Estate tax appraisals, Insurance claims appraisals, Divorce settlement appraisals, General appraisal services, Online appraisals, and In-person appraisals. Many also support authenticity research, market analysis, and sale preparation for private collectors and institutions.

How much does an art appraisal cost in Minneapolis, MN?

Fees vary by scope. Local providers reference Contact for pricing information, Starts at $150 per item (online), $300+/hour (in-person), and Typically $200–$500 per report (varies by complexity). Expect a written report that complies with USPAP standards and documents methodology, comparables, and appraiser credentials.
