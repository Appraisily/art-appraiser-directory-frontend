Frequently asked questions about Boston, MA appraisers

How do I choose an art appraiser in Boston, MA?

Look for professionals with ISA, ASA, or AAA credentials and USPAP-compliant reports. Start with our vetted directory, schedule a consultation, and ask about recent work that mirrors your collection.

What services do appraisers in Boston, MA provide?

Specialists in Boston, MA routinely assist with Personal Property Appraisals, IRS-compliant appraisals, Estate Planning, Fine Art Appraisals, Museum-grade Collection Valuations, and Art Appraisals. Many also support authenticity research, market analysis, and sale preparation for private collectors and institutions.

How much does an art appraisal cost in Boston, MA?

Fees vary by scope. Local providers reference Project-based with free photo-based assessments, Contact for pricing information, and Free consultation available; pricing varies by service. Expect a written report that complies with USPAP standards and documents methodology, comparables, and appraiser credentials.
