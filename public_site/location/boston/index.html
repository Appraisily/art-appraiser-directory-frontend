Frequently asked questions about Boston, Massachusetts appraisers

How do I choose an art appraiser in Boston, Massachusetts?

Look for professionals with ISA, ASA, or AAA credentials and USPAP-compliant reports. Start with our vetted directory, schedule a consultation, and ask about recent work that mirrors your collection.

What services do appraisers in Boston, Massachusetts provide?

Specialists in Boston, Massachusetts routinely assist with Art appraisal services, Insurance appraisals, Estate and donation appraisals, Authentication, Litigation Support, and Jewelry appraisal support. Many also support authenticity research, market analysis, and sale preparation for private collectors and institutions.

Fees vary by scope. Local providers reference Contact for pricing information, $350/hour or $2,500/day plus expenses, and Free consultation available; pricing varies by service.
